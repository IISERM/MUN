IISERM Model United Nations 2022

1st - 2nd October

Vision

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Mohali is one of India's most premier Undergraduate research-oriented Institutes, incubating some of the best minds in the country. Model United Nations is an educational simulation and an academic competition in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. The main watchword of IISER MUN will be to deliver the student community a forum for enriching their skills to create better individuals for the hereafter. 4 The inaugural edition is expected to be attended by several delegates from various prestigious schools and college . We hope to see you make the most of this opportunity to showcase your abilities to the world.
